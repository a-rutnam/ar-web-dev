About Me

link to resume here
I am a front-end developer with a particular interest in creating intuitive and beautiful websites that are a pleasure for users to peruse.

Through an immersive course at General Assembly I’ve definitely caught the web-development bug. The challenges of learning a new coding language are more than matched by the sense of accomplishment I feel when something works - whether that involves making the words ‘Hello World’ print in the browser, or building a website that required the user to complete physical exercises (recognised by the Microsoft Kinect) in order to read a news article.

I come from a background of working in office administration and sub-editing that has stood me in good stead for coding (especially when it comes to debugging). Prior to this I obtained a diploma in Applied Fashion, where I was always drawn to computer-aided design (CAD) both in developing clothing patterns and creating fashion illustrations.

I’m eager to keep learning and to become a contributing member of the dynamic developer community.
